About

Hi! I'm Lucy Jasons, the Photographer from New York and I have been doing photography since 2002 right after I qualified as a hairdresser. Taking random shots wherever I used to go to was part of my hobby until one day someone asked me why I don't do it for a business. It was the moment when I thought about it and realised that it is a great idea and I can turn my hobby into a job. Now I'm more than happy doing what I love. I take photos of people, nature, events, weddings, even animals if you like. I do have a little british short hair breed cat myself so not problem at all. Photography is absolutely everything to me and I will do my best to make everyone happy with their photos. I have also done a lot of courses with a photoshop so every photo can be turned into something very beautiful and magical. Something you will definitely love!
